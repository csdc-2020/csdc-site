Guidelines For Authorized Learning Center / Franchise

CSDC(COMPUTER & SKILL DEVELOPMENT CENTER) is a Mission to literate the masses in computer education. For This great mission Computer & Skill Development Center requires thousands of people. No great mission can Be fulfilled without the participation of the maximum number Of the peoples. No caption can win alone without his team.

First of all on Computer & Skill Development Center we must thank you and appreciate you for Your kind interest in Franchise enquiry of Computer & Skill Development Center. If you want to have Franchise in any District, City, village or Area, all over India then as per the requirements You must have 5-10 computers in your computer lab, sufficient place For the training of minimum 100-150 students (200-500 Sq. feet approx.), One theory room, one practical room, one reception, one office, also a library with 50 books.

To get affiliation Institute should have to submit an application form along with the authorization fees through Online (https://www.csdcin.com) or Demand Draft in favour of COMPUTER & SKILL DEVELOPMENT CENTER Payable at ICHAPUR and send it to our Head Office by email or Post.

WHAT YOU WILL GET?

  1. Agreement Paper.
  2. CSDCAuthorized ASC Certificate.
  3. Information Brochure and Registration form.
  4. Institute detail on our official Website………
  5. Website login to manage online registration, exams and accounts.
  6. Fax (without frame) of size 6 x3 with ALC Branding.
  7. Sample Mark sheet, Certificate, I-Card Book Notebook
