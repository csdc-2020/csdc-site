Registration Procedure

To get registered, student have to fill the application form provided along with information Brochure. Students can buy the information Brochure from any of the authorized Study center (ASC) and register in any of our CSDCAuthorized Learning Center. CSDC providesregular and University courses (only at selected ASCs). The ASC name and code are to be mentioned in the application form at the time of admission. The total fee has to be paid at the time of registration by Demand Draft favoring “Computer & Skill Development Center”.

Enclosures With The Application Form

  • Two recent photograph one passports and one stamp size.
  • Attested Photo copy of last educational qualification.
  • Category Certificate (SC/ST/ Handicapped) to be submitted to get relaxation in the fee as per the CSDCfee structure.

Course Structure

Considering the requirements of the aspirants, courses can be classified into

  • Certificate courses
  • Diploma/Advance Diploma courses. Former is basically designed for the beginners whereas the latter are for those who aspire to become high quality computer professionals.

Certificate Courses

These courses have been specifically designed to meet the current requirements of the IT Industry. The Certificate course is basically for the beginners who desire to start their careers in computer. CSDChas presented the course with nominal charges so that every section of the society can easily afford to learn the basics of computer & get benefited.

Diploma/Advance Diploma Courses

To prepare a new high qualified breed of computer professionals "Computer & Skill Development Center” has designed its diploma courses. These courses have been well thought out to meet the requirements of the fast growing IT industry in which Internet Programming, Advanced Programming, Accountancy, Desktop Publishing are being covered. Students can get benefited from a well premeditated course curriculum that matches the modern industry needs and emerges as a well skilled IT professional.
