Registration Procedure

The minimum qualifying marks for course/module are as follows – Practical 50 + theory 50 total 100

Course                                                              Term End Examination

Diploma Course                                  50%

Certificate Course                              50%

After qualifying all the exams, the students are awarded grades as per their percentage of marks obtained.

Grade System :

90% and above AA

80% and above A+

70% and above A

60% and above B+

50% and above B

40% and above C

Re examination

A student has to secure at least 50% marks in each course/semester to qualify the exam. A Student who does not clear the examination in one sitting will be allowed to re-appear in the failed exam. Re-examination fee of Rs 200/-has to be paid by Demand Draft favoring “Computer& Skill Development Center”. Students falling to appear in the re-examination within the specific period of time shall not be entitled for the certificate or for further re-examination. Such students are to go through the complete registration procedure to get a re-admission.

Examination Procedure

  • Examination will be conducted all around the year at your CSDCAuthorized Study Center. The pattern of exam will be of objective type.
  • Details i.e dates of examination, timing/session will be according to the time when the examination form was filled up. A login for 10 days would be open for ASC after filling examination form for the students to commence for the examinations. After that thestudent has to fill the examination form again with exam fees.
  • The duration of examination will be of 60 Minutes. Theory examination will comprise of 25 questions of 2 marks each, total 50 Marks. There is no negative marking.
  • Practical Exam will carry total 50 Marks.
